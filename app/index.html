CONTROLS

Hold left click and release to create a path.
To connect two paths, just start holding left
click near a path extremity and release
it near another.

Press A to add an input density flux to the
hovered path.

Press X to remove a hovered path.

Press I to invert a hovered path.

Note : The road generator only supports
up to 100 roads.
